Robotic Prosthetic Arm

Major Qualifying Project

The primary development of robotic hands focuses on strength over appearance and motion. The goal of this project is to replicate the figure and dexterity of a human hand and arm by demonstrations of American Sign Language (ASL) gestures. This was achieved by producing a programmable, cable-actuated robotic arm with a silicone external layer to perform dexterous movements accurate to human range of motion. This arm will eventually be implemented as part of a full humanoid robot, HURON
